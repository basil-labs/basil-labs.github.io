Measure Sentiments on Public Trust & Safety As Public Spaces Reopen

Introducing ELLA, a voice survey platform that allows patrons, visitors, and local residents to record and share voice surveys about public safety, and community concerns more efficiently. ELLA leverages Ai to review responses and converts voice notes into insightful dashboards that visualize topics and key takeaways. ELLA can analyze voice surveys in 120+ languages!

We tie this consumer engagement application with Catalyst, a mobility tracker that helps stakeholders better understand how pedestrians are traveling through open space and retail corridors in real-time.
